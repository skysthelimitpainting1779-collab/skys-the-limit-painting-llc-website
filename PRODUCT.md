# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Homeowners, commercial property managers, and public sector facility managers in the Twin Cities Metro Area looking for high-quality, owner-operated, prep-first elite painting and pavement marking services.

## Product Purpose
The website serves as the primary digital storefront, local search entity (SEO), validation of credentials, and high-converting lead intake channel.

## Brand Personality
High-end premium craftsman. Industrial luxury, highly structured, clean spatial layout. High-contrast typography, strict HSL palette (dark charcoal `#050505` text on safety orange `#FF5A00` accents), no emojis, and direct owner-operator accountability.

## Anti-references
Saturated AI cream/beige templates, generic 3-column Bootstrap-style card rows, fake reviews/ratings, and national franchise middleman styling.

## Design Principles
1. **Prep-First Verification**: Every visual and copy section must prioritize showing preparation craftsmanship (scraping, sanding, priming) before showing final paint.
2. **Owner-Operator Accountability**: Highlight that work is owner-led, insured, and verified.
3. **Structured Local Authority**: Use precise localized layout elements (maps, project cities) to validate Twin Cities Metro focus.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG level AA compliance, high contrast text on backgrounds (never use white text on safety orange), and smooth reduced-motion alternatives for Framer Motion.
